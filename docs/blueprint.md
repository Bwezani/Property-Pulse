# **App Name**: Property Pulse

## Core Features:

- Property Lifecycle Management: Manage properties from construction to rental, track details, and change statuses.
- Construction Management: Track costs, expenses, and completion status during construction, and trigger type updates when the property is marked finished.
- Rental & Income Management: Record rent payments, track payment status, and automatically calculate expected monthly payments. Show progress through visual investment recovery bar, tracking neutral, loss, or profit modes.
- Expense & Maintenance: Track ongoing property expenses like repairs, utilities, and cleaning, with categorized expenses and receipt attachments.
- Financial Reporting: Automatically calculate and generate reports for total investment, construction costs, rent received, remaining investment, and net profit. It can generate monthly income and expense summaries, construction cost summaries, and profit/loss reports.
- Sentiment Analysis Tool: Uses AI to scan descriptions from submitted expense records. The AI tool will analyze for unexpected cost overruns. An alert will appear in the financial report.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Forest green (#38A3A5) to represent growth and investment, evoking a sense of stability and prosperity.
- Background color: Pale green (#E7F5F5), providing a calm and unobtrusive backdrop that allows the primary elements to stand out without causing strain.
- Accent color: Warm orange (#FFB703) as an analogous color to green that provides a contrasting highlight for key actions and important notifications.
- Body font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font that ensures clear, accessible text throughout the dashboard and reports, making complex financial data easy to read.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a geometric sans-serif to maintain a contemporary, clean aesthetic for all titles and labels, complementing Inter's body text for an approachable user interface.
- Use consistent, clean icons throughout the platform to visually represent different property types, statuses, and financial metrics, aiding quick recognition and navigation.
- Dashboard designed with a clear grid system, with easily understandable tables, charts and progress bars to make it quick to grasp current investment status.
